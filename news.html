Lab News & Updates

December 2025

Grant Award for Genetic Heterogeneity Research

Our laboratory has been awarded a significant research grant to investigate genetic heterogeneity in rare genetic disorders. This funding will support three years of intensive research aimed at identifying novel disease genes and understanding mechanisms of genetic variation in human disease.

November 2025

Publication in Human Genetics Journal

Our latest study on genetic mapping and novel locus identification has been published. The research presents findings on a new DFNA locus for hearing loss, expanding our understanding of genetic heterogeneity in nonsyndromic hearing loss disorders.

October 2025

Collaborative Research Initiative

We have initiated a new collaborative project with international research institutions to investigate population genetics in South Asian populations. This collaboration will enhance our capacity for large-scale genomic studies and comparative population analysis.

September 2025

New Team Members Join Laboratory

We welcome new researchers to our laboratory. Our team now includes postdoctoral fellows, graduate students, and research associates who bring expertise in genomics, bioinformatics, and molecular genetics.

About HMG Lab

The Human Molecular Genetics Laboratory is dedicated to advancing understanding of genetic variation and its role in human disease.
